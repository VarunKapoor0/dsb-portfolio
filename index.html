About

Portrait

Dhruvraj Singh Bhati is a producer based in Los Angeles and a graduate of the USC School of Cinematic Arts. His work spans narrative shorts, documentary, and commercial production.

He is drawn to grounded, character-driven stories and to the producing problems nobody else wants: impossible locations, tight nights, and budgets that demand invention. He is currently producing independent shorts and taking on freelance production roles in Los Angeles.
